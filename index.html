WHO AM I?

The Story Behind Spirit2k5

Hey! I'm Carlton Mahlori Tshianeo, but the gaming world knows me as Spirit2k5. The name comes from my two biggest passions — gaming and gaming PCs / latest tech — combined with my birth year, 2005.

I was born on November 11, 2005 at the legendary Baragwanath Hospital in Soweto. I started my life in Chiawelo, living with my grandparents and my three uncles — Peter, Innocent "Pepe" (named after his insane football skills), and Elvis.

In 2016, I moved to a small village called Botsoleni with my grandparents, where I lived until 2023. That's when I moved back to Soweto, Meadowlands, to live with my parents and chase my dreams.

Gaming isn't just a hobby for me — it's a lifestyle. Whether it's grinding ranked on COD Mobile, causing chaos in GTA V Online, building the ultimate gaming rig, or keeping up with the latest tech drops, I'm always in my element.

"Not all gold and silver is a treasure."
